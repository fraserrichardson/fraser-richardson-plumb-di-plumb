04 Why Plum
"Grow money for life" is a clearer brand proposition than most fintechs manage. I'd rather work on that than on a positioning that hasn't been figured out yet.

Savings and investing into a consumer finance audience is a category where verbal identity genuinely does drive trust. Plum operates at European scale across ten markets — the sort of multilingual positioning problem I find interesting after four years of multilingual work out of Monaco.

Two gaps I should name. I have not led a brand refresh at the scale Plum is describing as an internal consultant to a Product Design team and a Creative Studio; the closest analogues are building the brand and content system at Savills and at FI Group from a much smaller starting point. And I have not held end-to-end relationships with external brand strategy and creative agencies at Plum's size. I'd expect to over-invest in the first month on reading the current brand system, the customer research you already hold and the agency relationships before proposing anything.

05 On AI

Part of the workflow, not a separate exercise.

Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney and lightweight automations sit inside drafting, research, landing-page iteration, brand-voice calibration and campaign analysis. I use them to hold a consistent tone of voice across a lot of output — close to the operational problem a Brand Lead overseeing creative at scale is trying to solve.
